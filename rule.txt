📜 *Word Quiz Game Rules* 🎲

1️⃣ Bot ek random letter choose karega aapke turn ke liye.

2️⃣ Aapko us letter se start hone wala *valid English word* likhna hoga (kam se kam 4 letters ka).

3️⃣ Aapke paas 20 seconds honge reply dene ke liye. Uske baad turn miss ho jayega.

4️⃣ Sahi jawab par aapko 1 point milega.

5️⃣ Turn players ke beech me round-robin chalta hai (baari baari).

6️⃣ Game end hone par highest score wale player ko *Winner* declare kiya jaayega! 🏆

❗ Galat ya invalid word par point nahi milta.

📝 *Game start karne ke liye:* `/quiz`
